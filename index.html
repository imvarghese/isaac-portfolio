Comet

Comet is a premium Indian sneaker brand with five stores across Bangalore, Delhi, Ahmedabad, and Hyderabad. Running a multi-city retail operation without a centralised tool meant stockouts discovered too late, decisions made on gut feel, and store managers calling each other to check stock. Built a full-stack inventory dashboard covering inbound POs, warehouse bin-level visibility, store health, and an AI demand planning layer with four forecasting models. The dashboard also includes Comet Intelligence: a Claude-powered chat assistant that takes a real-time snapshot of all inventory, sales rates, and inbound stock, and answers operational questions in plain English.

Valeo Health

Valeo Health had $20M in funding, clients like DEWA and Deloitte, and still ranked #2 on Google for at-home blood testing in Dubai. Yet they kept losing customers to competitors at the exact moment someone was ready to book. I dug into why. The problem was not visibility, it was trust signals and a split brand across two domains confusing the same buyer. Built a three-part roadmap: unify the brand, add a 48-hour WhatsApp nudge to generate Trustpilot reviews, and redesign landing pages to actually convert high-intent search traffic.
